MANUFACTURING OF METAL STRUCTURES

Design, manufacture and installation of metal structures for the frames of industrial and manufacturing buildings (warehouses, hangars, sheds, industrial premises, parking garages, sports facilities, gas stations, etc.).Installation of prefabricated buildings. More details on our website in the "Prefabricated buildings" section.Design and manufacture of various metal products: racks, columns, trusses, beams, stiffeners, girders, fences, platforms, consoles, stairs, etc.Strengthening of various building structures with metal (reinforced concrete columns, foundations, consoles, etc.).Manufacturing of metal products: gratings, fences, cages, garbage cans, etc.

All work is carried out in accordance with the drawings, in accordance with the metal assortment and specifications. It is possible to manufacture any metal structures and metal products in accordance with the wishes of the Customer and the developed technical documentation. We can perform calculations and develop drawings of any metal structures.
