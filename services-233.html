CONSTRUCTION

We are licensed to work in normal conditions and difficult geological conditions. We carry out reconstruction, new construction, mansard additions and major repairs. We offer a full range of repair and construction works from scratch to interior decoration and facade cladding. We act as a general contractor. We carry out the construction of buildings of any technological complexity on a turnkey basis, performing the following types of work.

We construct buildings using both traditional and modern construction technologies. On our website, you can learn about some of them, such as the Termodom technology and the Prefabricated Buildings technology.

  • Delimitation of a land plot
  • Legalization of the building
  • Design of structures
  • Real estate valuation
  • Plan of the land plot & more

In the offices of our Company, you can get detailed advice on design, design, documentation approval, construction, new technologies, as well as get acquainted with catalogs and samples of construction, finishing, facade and roofing products.
