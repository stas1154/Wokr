DESIGN

Designing is an important stage before starting construction! When designing an object, you can use a ready-made solution or develop an individual project. Already at the design stage, it is important to think about the future interior design, taking into account the placement of furniture and equipment, types of exterior, landscape features, take into account nearby buildings and structures, network connections, and much more. By contacting us, you get a comprehensive design!

The process of creating a full working draft, including all the necessary sections, is determined by the complexity of the building, its area, the number of project sections, and the approval process, and can take from 1 to 6 months. The use of standard solutions reduces the design time; for complex projects of large facilities with complex technological processes, the time depends on the design decisions made and the issuance of initial data by executive state bodies.

  • Delimitation of a land plot
  • Legalization of the building
  • Design of structures
  • Real estate valuation
  • Plan of the land plot & more

By agreement and power of attorney from the Customer, we can perform all types of work related to obtaining the initial data, as well as further project approval, including commissioning of the facility.
