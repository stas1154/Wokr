MODULAR HOUSES

This is a modern technology for the construction of buildings, structures, and private residential houses, when the building frame is made of lightweight metal. This technology has proven itself very well in the construction market. Mansard superstructures, lightweight hangars, prefabricated warehouses, industrial buildings, and entire cottage communities are being built using this technology. At the moment, the approximate cost of the kit is about $400-550 per 1 m² of the total building area. The cost is calculated individually using the program, depending on the area, number of storeys and complexity of the building's architectural design. After receiving the initial data in full, the final cost of construction and connection of utilities is specified.

The time for assembling a box house or building on the Customer's site is approximately 1-2 weeks for an area of up to 200 m², the installation time also depends on the area, number of floors and complexity of the building's architectural design. All earthworks and foundation works are carried out before the start of assembly. Only on the finished foundation are wall structures mounted and wall panels installed.
